Our Holiday Adventure Rafting

At Crossed Oars Adventure, our mission is to create unforgettable rafting experiences while connecting people with nature, teamwork, and adventure. We believe in safety, passion, respect for the environment, and the spirit of exploration. Our motto is: “Ride the Rapids, Live the Adventure.”

History

Crossed Oars Adventure was founded in 2012 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who shared a passion for rivers, exploration, and adventure tourism. What started as a small local rafting project quickly grew into a trusted company known for exciting expeditions, professional guides, and memorable outdoor experiences for families, friends, and travelers from around the world.
